How a trait becomes rare in the image

  1. Weight table — e.g. Hypno eyes weight 1, Normal weight 10.
  2. Score — sum of 1/weight across background, ground, plants, body, eyes, hat, mouth, extra.
  3. Tier cut — thresholds map score → Mythic / Legendary / Epic / Rare / Uncommon.
  4. Visual flex — ultra scores can upgrade solid skies to Gradient* paints so rarities look different, not just labeled different.
  5. Collision guard — exact 8-trait combos re-roll until all 999 DNA strings are unique.
